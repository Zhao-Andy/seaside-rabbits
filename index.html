Seaside Rabbits


Helping Farmed Rabbits to Loving Homes


Seaside Rabbits is a not-for-profit volunteer service which helps rabbits rescued from meat or fur farms to loving, indoor lives as family pets. Seaside Rabbits is not a shelter. Healthy, non-aggressive, spayed/neutered animals, who have potential to be great pets are identified. These animals have been rescued. Usually they are afraid of people and have no idea of how to live in an indoor home.

When Seaside takes a rabbit, it is carefully checked by an exotics vet, then brought to a home. It learns to socialize with people and stays for as many months as it needs to become adoptable. When the right adopter is found, the bunny goes to its new home.

Seaside Rabbits also gives small group education on house rabbit care, and provides support for rescued farm rabbits currently in shelters.
